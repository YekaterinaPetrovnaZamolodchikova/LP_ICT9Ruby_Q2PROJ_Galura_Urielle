Bistek Tagalog

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lbs beef sirloin thinly sliced
  • 5 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 4 pieces calamansi or 1-piece lemon
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 3 cloves garlic minced
  • 3 pieces onion sliced into rings
  • 4 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 pinch salt

Instructions

  1. Marinate beef in soy sauce, lemon (or calamansi), and ground black pepper for at least 1 hour.
  2. Heat the cooking oil in a pan, then pan-fry half of the onions until soft. Set aside.
  3. Drain the marinade from the beef. Pan-fry the beef for 1 minute per side. Remove and set aside.
  4. Sauté garlic and remaining onions. Pour the marinade and water, then bring to a boil.
  5. Add beef. Cover and simmer until tender. Add water as needed.
  6. Season with salt and pepper. Top with pan-fried onions. Serve hot.

Filet Mignon

Ingredients

  • 2 Filet Mignons
  • Salt & ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 5 cloves garlic peeled
  • 4 sprigs fresh rosemary

Instructions

  1. Season steaks and let sit at room temperature for 30-60 minutes.
  2. Heat a skillet over high heat. Add oil and sear steaks for 2-3 minutes per side.
  3. Add butter, garlic, and rosemary to the skillet. Bake at 360°F for 2-8 minutes, depending on doneness preference.
  4. Remove steaks, tent with foil, and let rest for 5-10 minutes. Serve.
