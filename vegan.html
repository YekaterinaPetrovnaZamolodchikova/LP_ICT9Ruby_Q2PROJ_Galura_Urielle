Enoki Chicken Wings

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon Sichuan peppercorns
  • 1 teaspoon sweet paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander seeds
  • 8 ounces (227 grams) enoki mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup (60 grams) all-purpose flour
  • 5 tablespoons (40 grams) cornstarch
  • 1/2 teaspoon (2 grams) baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon (3 grams) kosher salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Neutral oil for frying
  • 1/2 teaspoon (3 grams) fine sea salt
  • Ketchup, Japanese mayonnaise, or sweet Thai chili sauce (optional)

Instructions

  1. Toast Sichuan peppercorns, paprika, cumin, and coriander seeds. Grind into fine powder.
  2. Prepare enoki mushrooms by trimming roots and slicing thinly.
  3. Mix flour, cornstarch, baking powder, kosher salt, white pepper, garlic powder, and water into a smooth slurry.
  4. Heat oil to 350°F and deep-fry coated mushrooms until golden and crisp.
  5. Season with sea salt and mala spice. Serve immediately with optional dips.

Kimchi Salad with Noodles

Ingredients

For the Dressing

  • 1 garlic clove, crushed
  • 1/2 tablespoon ginger, finely grated
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoon tamari/soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoon tahini
  • 1 teaspoon Gojuchang paste (optional)
  • 2 tablespoon water

For the Salad

  • 270 grams Soba noodles
  • 1 large carrot, julienne
  • 2-3 spring onions, finely sliced
  • 2 1/2 cups cabbage, finely sliced
  • 2/3 cup kimchi, chopped

Instructions

  1. Whisk dressing ingredients together until smooth.
  2. Cook noodles per package instructions, drain, and rinse with cold water.
  3. Combine salad ingredients in a large bowl. Add noodles and dressing.
  4. Toss well and top with seeds before serving.
