Dessert Recipes

Tiramisu

Ingredients

  • 1 package ladyfingers
  • 4 eggs, separated
  • 1 cup sugar, divided
  • 1/4 cup rum, divided
  • 16 ounces mascarpone
  • 1 cup strong coffee
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Cocoa powder for dusting

Instructions

  1. Brew coffee and mix with 2 tbsp rum.
  2. Beat egg whites with 1/2 cup sugar until stiff.
  3. Whisk egg yolks with remaining sugar, mascarpone, vanilla, and 2 tbsp rum.
  4. Layer coffee-dipped ladyfingers and mascarpone mixture in a pan.
  5. Dust with cocoa and refrigerate for 4+ hours before serving.

Leche Flan

Ingredients

  • 10 eggs (yolks only)
  • 1 can condensed milk (14 oz)
  • 1 cup fresh milk or evaporated milk
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Beat egg yolks in a bowl.
  2. Mix in condensed milk, fresh milk, and vanilla extract.
  3. Caramelize sugar in a mold and spread evenly.
  4. Pour in the egg mixture, cover with foil, and steam for 30-35 minutes.
  5. Cool, refrigerate, and serve chilled.
